The Importance of Kindness

04 Aug 2023

Never miss an Opportunity to be Kind

I saw this video shortly after I woke up this morning and sent the link to family and friends. I have never met a person who regretted being kind, especially to those who don’t deserve it. As I listened to this, I remembered seeing my dad, who disappeared when I was 4 and showed up again when I was almost 20. I had no interest in seeing him, but decided to be kind. It was a great decision, which I have never regretted. I never saw him again, but that encounter was about the sort of person I was, not who he had been.
