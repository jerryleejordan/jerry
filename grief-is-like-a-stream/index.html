Grief is like a Stream

05 Jan 2024

View this post on Instagram

A post shared by Gerald Lee Jordan (@therapyaroha)

My mum died on Boxing Day, at about the time I was reading this book and posting this quote to Instagram. I knew she would die soon, but thought there might be about another week. I was waiting for Tracy outside of a party she had gone to and was using my time reading. I didn’t realise how close the grief truly was.

I miss my mum - terribly, deeply.
